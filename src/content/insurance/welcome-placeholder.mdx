## Why standard auto policies fall short

Most personal auto policies were written with stock vehicles in mind. When you add conversion components — beds, electrical systems, plumbing, solar — you introduce value the underlying policy was never priced for.

<CalloutBox type="key" title="The short version">
If your insurer doesn't know what's bolted to the inside of your van, it's very likely uninsured.
</CalloutBox>

## What to ask any carrier

- Will the conversion value be covered on a stated-value or agreed-value basis?
- Are personal belongings inside the van covered while parked?
- Is full-time occupancy allowed or excluded?
